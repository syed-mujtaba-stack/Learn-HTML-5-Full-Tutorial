WebRTC & Media Capture - Exercise

1. Accessing the Webcam

Use navigator.mediaDevices.getUserMedia to access the webcam and display the video stream below.

Hint: Request video stream only, and set the srcObject of the video element.

2. Take a Snapshot

Add a button to capture a snapshot from the webcam and display it as an image.

Hint: Draw the video frame onto a <canvas> and use toDataURL().

3. Basic Peer Connection (Loopback)

Set up a simple RTCPeerConnection (loopback) that connects to itself and displays the remote stream in another video element.

Hint: Create two RTCPeerConnection objects, connect their ICE candidates, and exchange offers/answers locally.
